The problem

Karting is a sport. Almost nobody trains for it.

If you've ever felt your neck give up mid-stint or your forearms pump up until you can't feel the wheel, you already know the problem.

1

Generic plans miss the point

Standard fitness programmes don't train lateral neck strength, grip endurance under vibration, or bracing against g-forces — the exact things karting demands.

2

No specialized guidance

Amateur drivers train alone and guess. Advice written for F1 athletes doesn't fit someone with a job, a family and six track days a year.

3

Coaching is out of reach

Professional motorsport trainers exist — at professional prices. There's nothing affordable between "nothing" and "hire a performance coach".

The solution

Personalized preparation, built for your season

TrackReady is digital, affordable, and specific to motorsport — designed for drivers who train independently.

1

Digital assessment

Tell us about your events, training history and where you fatigue first. It takes 10 minutes.

2

Your training plan

A personalized programme targeting neck, core, grip and stint conditioning — matched to your schedule and equipment.

3

Recovery guidance

Race-weekend routines, between-stint habits, and deload weeks timed around your events.

4

Progress tracking

Simple weekly measures — including the one that matters: which lap fatigue shows up on.
